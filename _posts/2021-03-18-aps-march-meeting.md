I'm at APS March Meeting all the time these couple of days. The online platform is actually pretty efficient.

1. You can open couple of meeting rooms and monitor all of them at the same time. Compared to the in-person meeting, where you have to stand up, walk out with everybody, including the presenter, watching you, open the door, which will most likely make some noise — now, going from room to room is much easier.

2. You can ask questions anonymously. This makes me feel much easier when asking some questions which I'll deem as too simple, or too bold, when asking in person.

I hope in the future, there can always be an option to attend the meeting online.
